Wondering what to do with your food waste?

It doesnt quiet fit in the trash can with everything else. And yet you cant seem to find a way to recycle it.


Our solution is a smart bin, Smarty where you can throw in all your food waste, find out how much, in kilos, you are throwing away, and track it all on your phone on our Smarty Tracker app.


You don't have to worry about where it goes. We collect it all, and turn it into compost.
Who knows, perhaps the next time you eat, it could be food grown from your contribution.
